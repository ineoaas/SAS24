About Us

SAS24 Supply and Services

SAS24-LLC (Supply and Services 24 Hours) specialises in the design, manufacturing, and sales of heating blankets, drum heaters, and insulated drum jackets.

Continuing the legacy of Grand Engineering Supply (2012–2021), SAS24 is led by an expert with 30+ years of field experience in GRE heating applications, especially for fiberglass pipe installation in the oil and gas sector.

Our products are engineered in the UAE and manufactured by trusted partners in China, combining innovation with cost-efficiency. We also serve the chemical, oil & gas, and food industries, providing reliable solutions for heating viscous materials.

All products are CE and RoHS certified, QC-tested in the UAE, and come with a unique serial number and Material Test Certificate (MTC), ensuring safety, compliance, and traceability.

We offer custom-built solutions, backed by a dedicated in-house engineering team and an evolving R&D department that responds to client needs and market demands.

Heating Blankets for GRE Fiberglass Piping

Heating blankets are electrical heaters used for fiberglass pipe joint curing, made of special rubber with integrated temperature control thermostats.

Our GRE heating blankets, designed for GRE, GRP, RTR, and FRP pipes, provide a consistent heat source for adhesive curing. Our heating blankets are equipped with controllers that regulate temperatures from 20°C to 200°C, with custom temperature settings available upon client request.

Besides being used for GRE pipe and laminate joint curing, the blankets are also used for heating liquid drums and barrels.

Temperature Range20°C to 200°C (custom settings available)
Max Pipe DiameterUp to 1,800mm (72") or even larger
ControllerIntegrated variable; optional digital upon request
IP RatingIP-65, dust and waterproof
MaterialHigh-quality U.S. silicone
Shapes AvailableCircular, rectangular, square, and custom
Pipe TypesGRE, GRP, RTR, FRP

Drum & IBC Tank Heating Jackets

We manufacture and supply drum heater insulated jackets and IBC-tank heater insulated jackets of all sizes for all purposes. We have a long history of supply, and the customer can rely on our experience when it comes to customised solutions.

The drum/IBC heating jackets are frequently used for mixing at specific temperatures and to keep the temperature fixed at the desired set point.
